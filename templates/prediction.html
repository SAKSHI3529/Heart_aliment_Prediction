Attributes Information

Chest pain

Chest pain is discomfort caused when your heart muscle doesn't get enough oxygen-rich blood.

Rest BP

Ideal resting blood pressure is considered to be between 90/60 mmHg and 120/80 mmHg.

Chol

A high serum cholesterol level is a concern. Ideal serum cholesterol is considered to be less than 150 mg/dL.

FBS

A fasting blood sugar level of 99 mg/dL or lower is normal. (> 120 mg/dL, 1 = true; 0 = false)

Rest ECG

0 = Normal, 1 = ST-T wave abnormality, 2 = Probable left ventricular hypertrophy

MaxHR

To estimate your maximum age-related heart rate, subtract your age from 220.

Exang

Exercise-induced angina indicates the body is trying to pump more blood but cannot do so effectively. (1 = yes, 0 = no)

Oldpeak

ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest. 'ST' relates to positions on the ECG plot.

Slope

The slope of the peak exercise ST segment (1: Upsloping, 2: Flat, 3: Downsloping)

Ca

Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy.

Thal

Blood disorder thalassemia: Normal, Fixed (no blood flow), or Reversible Defect (blood flow observed but not normal).
